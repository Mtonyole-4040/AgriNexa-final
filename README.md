# 🌱 AgriNEXA AI

**AgriNEXA AI** is an emotionally intelligent, AI-powered farming assistant designed to guide farmers through the entire agricultural journey — from planning and planting to harvesting and selling — while also supporting farmer wellbeing and financial protection.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

AgriNEXA is built to solve real challenges faced by farmers:

* Uncertainty in crop selection
* Climate and weather risks
* Poor financial planning
* Market unpredictability
* Lack of emotional and mental support

AgriNEXA uses AI to transform farming into a guided, data-driven, and emotionally supported experience.

---

## 💡 Key Features

### 🤖 AI Onboarding Assistant

Farmers interact with AgriNEXA AI, which collects key data such as:

* Location
* Crop interest
* Farm size
* Budget
* Emotional state

This creates a personalized **Farmer Intelligence Profile**.

---

### 🌾 Smart Farming Recommendations

* AI suggests the best crops based on region and conditions
* Provides real-time farming insights
* Guides decision-making step by step

---

### 💰 Profit Prediction

* Estimates expected revenue and profit
* Helps farmers plan budgets effectively
* Supports smarter investment decisions

---

### 🌦 Weather Intelligence

* Predicts weather risks
* Advises farmers on optimal timing
* Reduces climate-related losses

---

### 📷 Crop Disease Scanner

* Allows farmers to scan crops
* AI detects potential diseases early
* Suggests preventive actions

---

### 🛡 AgriNEXA Farm Shield (Insurance)

* Optional farm protection system
* Covers:

  * Climate damage
  * Pest outbreaks
  * Crop failure
* AI recommends when protection is needed

---

### 💗 Farmer Wellbeing AI (Unique Feature)

AgriNEXA is not just about crops — it supports the farmer too.

* Tracks emotional state
* Provides mental support guidance
* Reduces stress and uncertainty

---

### 🌍 Market Intelligence

* Shows best markets for crops
* Predicts demand trends
* Helps farmers sell at the right time

---
